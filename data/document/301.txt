Avamar : Re-running Failed jobs from activity window	

Customers need to go to policies  or backup  and restore window to re- run failed backup
While working with customers new to avamar take time to  find polies and groups or find difficulty in running backups from there  
enabling  backup re-run option in activity window ,

By enabling run backup from activity window , helps users not go to specific client policy and re-running backup .
Helps L1 team or a person new to avamar  to re-run the backup .
Saves time in finding current policy  and group
