Cloud Computing Advertisement Project

NA
Create a market Ad that would encourage small business leaders and entrepreneurs to move to the cloud service.

Advertisement specs:
Ad duration: ~1 minute
Ad content: 
1- Advantages of Cloud over client-server model
2- Characteristics
3- Service Models (convenience)


Contest Specs:
Duration: 3 weeks
Groups: 3 to 4 students per group
Structure:
Week1: Research 
Week2: Formulate Ad content
Week3: Create and develop the Ad

Winning Criteria:
1- Demonstrate the cloud concepts in the best way
2- Creativity and innovation
3- Consider target audience clearly
