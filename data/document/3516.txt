Power Smart Analytic Software

Idea came up to enhance the power consumption by EMC Products.
- An Application S/W will monitor the power consumption of EMC Products.

- Power Consumption Report will be sent to EMC 

- EMC Big Data Anlysis solution will analyze this information and get the way to enhance it.

- EMC Will send reports to the customer to give him some tips to enhance the power consumption.

- EMC Will determine the Accuracy of the power consumption.

- Measure the efficiency of a Architecture design.
