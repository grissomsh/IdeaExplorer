Gameunity
This app will increase employee engagement and involvement.
By experience, competitions already held increase employee engagement. Increases fun, enthusiasm.
Gameunity is a mobile app for EMC employees; that serves online gaming across all countries.

Employees can download the app to their phones and start competing.
Competitions can be in many aspects: Examples (not limited to)
- General Information (instant competition between 2 members)
- EMC Technology (instant competition between 2 members)
- Board games (ex. monopoly)
- Paper based games (ex. Tic Tac Toe)
- Ongoing competitions by teams/BUs
- Monthly winner competition (ex. picture of the month)

Other app features:
- Member profile 
- Social discussions
- Posts (pictures, videos,...etc)

Building a community inside the EMC community will increase employee engagement and provide a more fun environment with a healthy competition
