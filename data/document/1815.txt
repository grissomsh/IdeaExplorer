Transformation to Mobile - EDU Portal
User Experience enhancement platform 3.0 direction and mindset
The increase of the mobile transformation need
The idea is to have a mobile application of https://education.emc.com/ for employees to be able to access their online trainings from their phones/tables.

This will increase employee engagement and by giving the ability to attend their online trainings from anywhere.

The app can cover employee transcript with privilege of registering in trainings as well.
This feature will make life easier as well as increase employee engagement.
