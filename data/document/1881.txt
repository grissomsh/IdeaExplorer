Silent Mode selector Mobile App
IT Contemporary Workplace Challenge

This App reduces the noise and disturbance during meetings which is generally not accepted by most of them.
We were part of a group that attended the ideas mining session conducted inhouse. While brainstorming the ideas we came up with this idea.
If exchange server synced on smart phone. Whenever there is a meeting appointment for the entire time frame this app will put the phone in silent mode.
Complete App should be developed and make it available for all EMC people.
This would help each one of especially who forget to put their phone in silent mode.
This would definitely help everyone including sales team when they are engaged with customer interaction.
