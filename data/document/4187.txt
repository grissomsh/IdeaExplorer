Data Mining and Knowledge Management in JIRA/Perforce Help Improve Product Quality and Customer Service

In the past, often engineers found that they spent long time to dealing with a certain problem which someone has met before. However, there is no easy way find out how the problem was resolved before. JIRA and Perforce contains some history information, we need a good way to digging out valuable information from JIRA and Perforce's large amount data.
Data mining in JIRA and perforce data. The data mining program may collect below data:
1) quality metrics of a product, like what kind of problems the product has most, which component has most problems. This can give us opportunity to improve the product quality.
2) problem's solution. This will help it easier to find out how a problem was resolved in the past. Developer's or support engineer can leverage this to resolve problems quickly.
