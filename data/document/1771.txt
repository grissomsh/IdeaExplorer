Charitable donations at Source
Simplify the process of donating to charities directly through a self-managed portal allowing employees to nomination a regular donation to an approved charity.  
Managing your charitable donations can often be difficult task as you often forget who you donate to and how much you provision.  If I was able to have a single location to see who I can/do donate money to and manage these donations directly from source it would both encourage and support my charitable contributions.
Online Charity Donation Portal. Provides an online web portal where EMC employees can apportion a specified monetary amount from their salary to one or more nominated charities.  
One Portal to manage charitable donations directly from payroll source.  I can manage contributions to approved charities from a single location without the hassle of organizing direct debit mandates.  Whatever tax rebates are afforded by national tax laws can be centrally managed and distributed
