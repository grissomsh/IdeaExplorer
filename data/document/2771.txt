Find Similar Documents with Z-Tree
This idea goes to Enterprise Content Division (ECD) Challenge. 
Finding documents similar to a target document is a very useful feature for content management. 
Most products try to find similar documents using search engine, like Lucene MoreLikeThis.
Lucene MoreLikethis is based on search and doesn't ensure to return most similar documents first.
This idea ensures documents with the maximum matching key words will be returned first.
Please refer to this document about this idea: 
https://inside.emc.com/docs/DOC-152130

This idea can be built into a brand new product and sold to customer to make money.
