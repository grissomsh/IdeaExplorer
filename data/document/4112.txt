Collecting Cigarette Butts

Innovation Session
Curtis Baffico, a San Diego stock trader who moonlights as an environmentalist, decided to create a recycling system for cigarette butts. One of his recycling ideas: Grind up the butts and add them to concrete: website, Ripplelife.org.
Anyway EMC can only collect those cigarette butts and deliver them to environmentalists who actually reuse them to produce something healthy.
