Car Ad-Hoc Network
auto connect neighboring cars and communicate with other cars.

The idea is discussed at a group meeting.
1.auto create network with neighboring cars.
2. sharing information, for example, can get your front car information(car speed),is there a car at the front of your front car? what type of the car?
3. communication, if you want turn left, you can notify your front, left and behind of your car. Also you can direct talk to one car which in your network.
This is for smart city and can avoid traffic accidents
