Parking Levels
The idea help in creating a better place to work in by eliminating the hassle happened everyday because of car blocking and no place to park in.
The idea came after seeing many problems happened because of people blocking other's cars and no places to park in. the idea of the parking levels is done in many places like the malls, huge buildings, Hyper markets and others
The idea is building lower levels in B2 parking or upper levels in the G area this could save many parking slots for employees to park in.
If it implemented it will save time and effort. will increase the satisfaction rate. will reduce the problems and no more cars blocking.
