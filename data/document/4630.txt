EMC Academy

students dream about their future job and this will increase their awareness about EMC and corporate life.
each group of students will make an imaginary company , each 2/3 students will be responsible for a department and they will be given a real case study in EMC.They will have to work on the case and make a presentation infront of EMC's Managers.
there will be selection phases in order to enter the competition to find the best candidates ,also to get the maximum output sessions can be held in different modules related to the case study
