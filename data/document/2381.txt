Instant User feedback for applications
A full proof mechanism to capture and act of end user or customer feedback within the application
The background of the idea is around empathizing with the customers or end users as part of the Design Thinking way of doing things
The solution is inline with Design Thinking concept by allowing the developers to empathize with the end user through a Embedding a customer feedback capturing mechanism within all the applications. The user can then provide instant feedback while using the application which the development team and then act upon
The major benefit is bridging the gap between what the customer wants to what is being developed by the application. The goal is to move towards a process were there will be little or no training required to use an application.
