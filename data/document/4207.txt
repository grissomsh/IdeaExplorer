Employee 3rd Degree of Seperation

Innovation Session
Competition in EMC COEs to get people to know each other more by getting the person who has most connections to other EMC employees (with maximum 3 levels of sepration but not through other EMC Employees) to win a prize. That will encouarage all employees to talk to each other and get know ach other which in turn will create a friendly and cozy environment and thus a greater place to work.
