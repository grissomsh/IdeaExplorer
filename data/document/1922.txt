Save Trees
It helps to avoid printing Papers/Paper wastage and in turn help to save trees and build better environment.
As daily user of office transport/routing list came up with this idea to avoid printing and saving trees.
Instead of using paper we can use a small projector post route list. It will have better visibility than paper and last minutes change can be posted online and above all it saves lot trees and builds awareness. 
Avoids printing.
Saves trees and builds Better environment.
Builds environment awareness in employees and drivers and will be motivating.
Better route list visibility and avoid crowds near routing list.
Last minute change can be done online.
