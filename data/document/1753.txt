Crowdsourcing App
This app encourage crowdsourcing in a fun, interesting way.
The idea came from the thought about hoe to engage employees in EMC projects. 
The App will Support: Photo sharing, Voting, Posting.

What is different about this APP is that it will support competition spirit as Employees will vote for the best ideas and the winners will have prizes.


This App will encourage Employees to engage in EMC business projects and this will be a kind of motivation and increasing trust between EMC and it's employees.
