Using the age of empires idea to promote a concept of building up a data center. 

Brainstorming session.
Similar to the game the student must start off at the lowest level and build up a data center using EMC products and solutions from the low end of EMC technology range up to the high end. The data center can then ascend into the Cloud arena. 
This can be an EMC APP rolled out to students in schools or even outside of the school environment.
