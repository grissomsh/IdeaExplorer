EBC ready CTD products solution => CTD|One
We are addressing the challenge of CTD supportability of multiple CTD products solutions found in one place
In the past EBC demos every product solution team had to to start setting up their demo setup atleast a day prior to EBC. 
This solution will make sure we have a single set-up with all the product solutions to be demoed during EBC without the need for setting up separately for every product solution.
1. Less time consumption as every solution is found in a single place.
2. People wont waste time setting up for every other product solution.
3. Same setup can be re-used for every demo just with a upgraded version
