System Monitoring

There is no monitoring of the health of the system provided.
For example, Graphical representation of the CPU utilization, memory, Process details.

Add plugin or open source monitoring tools to the appliance to provide system level details.
This will also tell us the utilization of the system resources.
If more resources are needed we can proactively increase the system configs as well.

- Customer knows whats happening in the system and avoid production down situation. 
- Keep track of the history of the system.
