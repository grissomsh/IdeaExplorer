Remote Proactive Mobile App

Customers and Remote proactive engineers alike need an easier way of checking the assigned activities along with all the details. 
A simple mobile app that will be installed on the mobile phone and can be used to provide access for the customer or engineer.

Provide real time updates to customers and engineers alike so that they are able to check what activities are scheduled along with all the associated details.

Different levels of access can be implemented to provide tiered access for managers, engineers, field, partners, and customers.
