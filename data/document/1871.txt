Meeting recorder
IT Contemporary Workplace Challenge

Irrespective of whether we have started recording on the webex this product records the meeting and puts the recorded file in a centralized server location.
I had a personal experience when i missed recording a demo which i had to repeat again for next set of audience where the set up was tough for me to do and took lot of my time.
That is when it clicked for me to have such product. During ideas mining session this ideas was further built up by the team.
Meeting recoder records all the webex mettings irrespective of the choice on the webex. The recorded file will be saved under a folder which is named after the meeting title and timestamp of the meeting. This folder will be accessed only to the invitees of the meeting.
The advantage of having this product will definitely enhance productivity.
