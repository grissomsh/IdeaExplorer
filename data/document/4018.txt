STEM trendy

Idea from brainstorming session with Martin O’Flaherty in EMC Ballincollig

The challenge will be conducted in two stages.
Stage 1: To gather students’ ideas of what they finds is interesting through the use of IT technology, innovation, big data, Cloud computing etc. Students at this stage are to upload ideas in to cloud that will apply use of it. The best three teams will move to Stage 2. Team have to has include boys and girls.
Stage 2: Teams will cooperate with EMC teams. EMC will create a ‘research centres’ that will support, manage and focus sessions with students. EMC team will carrying out specific forms of counselling, learning and development, aimed at encouraging young people – especially girls – to scientific careers. EMC teams with students will cooperate with universities: CIT and UCC in Cork.
