Smartphone Friendly Plant Interaction Points

Having walked through the plant and across the manufacturing floor the idea came to me when I began noticing the various different processes in manufacturing the product and the assortment of equipment and technology used in the process. In a moment of curiosity I thought it might be a good idea to have a means of being able to discover and learn more about EMC there and then.
QR codes or NFC points placed around the plant that users could interact with using their smartphone that will result in them being shown a video clip, web site or snippet of interesting information about the area they are currently standing in.
