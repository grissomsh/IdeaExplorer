SMS Notifications for Critical Alarms - New Alerting Channel For EMC Products
Targeting to improve EMC products and increase the customer satisfaction.

For all EMC products, In the Alarms configuration section, we will develop the alarms notification module to integrate with SMS Center or SMS Gateway, and send SMS notifications to the Customer Administrators once certain defined alarms are raised.
