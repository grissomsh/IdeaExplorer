Smart Car Tracking system
Implementing this idea will use technology to keep cars more secure and help approach smartcity
Seeing a lot of people losing their cars recently.
Adding a hidden chip in the car attached with GPS tracker. The chip will be connected to internet through a SIM card while its connected all the time to a server.

Car owner will have access to the application to detect location of his car.

Features:
1. Locate car
2. Stop car's engine
3. Detect cars speed
4. Listen to sounds inside the car in case it was stolen
Saving peoples money in case they were in risk of losing their car.
Making it easy to find your car if you park in large garage/space.
