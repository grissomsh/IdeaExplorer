HR Forum for EMC Employees

Ask HR tool. What if I have poor internet Connection and I want to urgently  request for an HR letter. 
Create an Application that enables EMC employees to ask HR using their mobile phones.Employees will have to register using their NT Logins to be able to connect to the app.

In this forum, employees can ask their questions according to the topic that is related to their need. For each Topic there is a discussion board where employees will ask whatever they want in form of posted messages.
