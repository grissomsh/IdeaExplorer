Consolidated EMC Software  
This idea will enhance customer's usage for EMC software tools to manage EMC's products.
NA 
A Software product that consolidates all the products of EMC that the customer purchased that shows the whole environment of the customer.

This software can send Alerts to the customer if something went wrong with any product of his environment.This software can include a support system when an alert occurs ,it automatically open a chat or create an SR with the support Team to investigate the issue.
1-Improved Management for EMC product from one interface.
2-Consolidated software for managing and monitoring customer's environment for all EMC Products.
