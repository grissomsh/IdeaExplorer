CSR Activities to be incorporated by EMC

The supported NGO should registered under 80(C) and should operate with orphanage/Drug Rehabilitation centre/Old Age home. The under-privileged would get the needful support.
EMC's 5000 employee contributing Rs. 1000/- will conclude an amount of Rs. 50,00,000/-. This Rs. 50,00,000/- can support an NGO to sustain for a year.

NGO operating orphanage/Drug Rehabilitation centre/Old Age home can sustain for the entire year.
