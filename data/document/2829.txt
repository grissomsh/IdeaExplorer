Green Roof
This Idea is directly related to cost of operations.  
The implementation of green roofs is a movement that is picking up steam across the country.  With EMC's commitment to Sustainability, it seems like a good fit.
Green Roofs have been shown to reduce the cost of heating and cooling of buildings by a significant amount while Reducing the buildings Carbon Footprint
The tools of the EMC factories would be covered in sustainable low maintenance plant life.
