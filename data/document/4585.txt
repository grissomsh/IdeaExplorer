Booking a meeting room in EMC COEs

It takes us time to find a free meeting room every time we want to reserve one. We have to check schedule of these rooms by name using microsoft outlook.

A mobile application that is connected to a server where information of all meetings rooms are stored.

Features:

-You can check for free rooms.

-You can get info about occupied room and what is it occupied for.

-You can book a room for desired time.

-You can book a meeting and invite any other employees.

-*Application can be integrated with other EMC applications.

-*Each employee's schedule can be integrated with mobile's calender.
