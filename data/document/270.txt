Request Me Application For Internal Hiring 
This will serve the challenge/s listed below. 

NA
Managers and team leads can request to hire internally an employee and move a him/her into their teams. 
For example, if there is a vacancy in the licensing team; the licensing team manager can request to hire a specific candidate that is currently in another team but she sees him fit for the new licensing role. 

A request is sent from the manager who is hiring to the individual employee they want to hire and to his direct manager to approve. Then, an interview is scheduled.
