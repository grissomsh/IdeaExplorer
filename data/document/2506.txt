Reaching for your laptop each time you want to navigate through your PPT while presenting is annoying and time consuming.

Using one of several iOS or Andriod free apps that converts your smartphone to a presenter, through bluetooth or wifi, instead of buying a presenter.
Examples: Mouse Kit (iOS), PowerPoint controller (Android)
It takes no time to implement, it's free, and it will make presenting easier.
