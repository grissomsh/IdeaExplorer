Using Big Data Analysis to predict future diseases 
This solution can be presented to healthcare organizations to present this service to their patients. This idea will address global solutions challenge.
The idea came from the thought about the time taken to diagnose a patient and the money spend to archive the medical history of previous patients
The idea is to make use of all the previous patients data and run a predictive algorithm to predict future diseases that could occur to current patients .
This idea will make a lot of money and save a lot of time for medical organizations.
