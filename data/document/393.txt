Management tool for all Service Requests
We will be providing a tool that will help customers to manage and keep record of their EMC service requests.

This is going to increase customer's satisfaction and experience with EMC.
Some customers complains about the frequent number of SRs and that they get confused between products.
Creating a stand-alone app which is connected to EMC Service Center. Application will be filtering SRs based on the product type.
For example: 150 SR for Isilon, 60 SR for Networker and 30 SR for Connectrix.

This will include status of each SR, Severity, creation date and closure date. Product can be listed as well per serial number if needed.

Note: Customers can get scheduled monthly report for their EMC SRs.
-Saving customers time and effort
-Making customers have great experience dealing with EMC support cases.
