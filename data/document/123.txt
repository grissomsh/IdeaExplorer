The Global Team Day
This will serve the challenge/s listed below. 

Team building events. 
Create customized T-shirts for every team such as CST team, Isilon team..etc.
All the team members would wear the same T-shirt on an agreed upon day globally and take pictures and share it with the other team members that are in differnt areas around the world. 

Example: all CSTs around the world would wear this agreed upon and previously designed and ordered T-shirts for the day and would share their team pictures. 
Creating a sense of pride and that each one is part of a global team
Team building and motivation
employee satisfaction
creative thinking and bringing people closer - diversity and no barriers
