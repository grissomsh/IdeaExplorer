Energy Production Desks
This idea will help the Employees having better health and will enable energy production at the same time.
I saw This idea implemented in one of the multinational companies , and it was admired by the employees.
My idea is Desks that has a bicycle  chair , where employees can enjoy cycling while doing their work,These bicycle in connected to a circuit that produces energy that can be used to operate the COE Electricity. 
1-Supports Physical Exercises for Employees during their working hours.
2-Energy production.
