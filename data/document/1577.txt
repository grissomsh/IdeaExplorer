Innovation Roadmap Filtering tool 
This idea will facilitate the process of filtering the Showcase ideas for the Innovation Roadmap.
The idea came up from reading other submitted ideas from the innovators that is irrelevant to the challenge or that is very poor and don't count as an idea.
This idea will use sentiment analytic technology to filter all the ideas and exclude the ones that has poor wording or quality or that is irrelevant to the challenge , instead of going through each idea one by one.
1-Facilitate the process for the sponsors to filter the ideas.
2-Decrease the time spent to filter the ideas.
