Certificates Exchange Portal 
This idea will help employees to exchange information for certificates they achieved.
This idea came up when i try to study for a new certificate and i am not sure from where to start or whom to ask when i get stuck in certain parts.
My idea is a portal for Employees who achieved certain certificates that got their contact information , where the employees that needs to achieve the same certificates can contact them.

It can also contains comments from the employees that achieved these certificates about the important parts to focus on and share the common questions that may come in the exams.
1-Knowledge Exchange between Employees.
2-Getting to know different people from different business units.
