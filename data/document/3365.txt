Inside EMC achievements

Ideation session
The employee can get more acheivements and badges by being more active on the site. For example and achievement for most number of posts or contributions, or another achievement if his/her answer is chosen as "Best Answer". If the employe gets a certain amount of "Best Answers", for example 100, a special award can be given: "Most Trusted". There can be Most Trusted Employees for different products, and that way other employees know who best to ask for these products. So in essence, they are EMC Champions chosen by the community.
