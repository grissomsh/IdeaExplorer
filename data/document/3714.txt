Emergency Medical Kit

- Health issues have become too common these days, this vitaly affects our employee health and business operations. In order to manage minor health issues, we should be having effective solution in place, hence the new solution proposition given.
- Get the Emergency Medical Kit, in every floor(s) BU Admin office and Security Front office. (Here, we have Kits available only at BU Admin Office).

- If the Floor area is too large, then consider to place the Medical Kit in Floor Cafeteria or with well-known Floor Warden, so that  it helps to locate easily.

- Bring Awareness in each team about availability of Medical Kit in respective places at Floor.

- Kit should contain, not just Gelusil, Cotton, Dettol. It should also contain other generic medicines for Headache, Pain relief, acidity etc, with appropriate tags.
