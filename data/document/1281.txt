Enhancing the customer experience with EMC.
I believe this aims to improve the overall process with the customer which in turn has a great effect on business.
Some customers come with a pre-judgement in a previous bad experience, and I think if this happened to him again, he would lose his faith in EMC. 
Having a Board for each customer and this board is visible to the WFMs, It contains the previous experience of the customer where he had a positive, negative or neutral experience (through surveys).

If he had a negative experience the WFM would assign the best available resources so that he feels the extra attention.
