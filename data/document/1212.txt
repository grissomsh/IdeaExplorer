Create a crowdsourcing hub for EMC employees
Solicit contributions from across the organization to help sub-divide tedious or repetitive tasks, or help solve problems too large for a single department or group
Came from reading about various crowdsourcing projects on the internet. One of the earliest examples of crowdsourcing dates to the 18th century call the Longitude Prize (https://longitudeprize.org/history) 
A crowdsourcing hub could help provide a way for various individuals or groups to come together and collaborate to find a solution. Crowdsourcing has proved to be a successful mechanism for organizations to solve problems that would have been to great to solve limited to their own resources.
Web portal to allow groups to register challenges and to contribute/submit ideas or solutions to particular challenges. Groups whose idea is accepted and implemented could be rewarded
