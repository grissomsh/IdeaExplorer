Project details in Propel
Challenge here is looking for every details of a PS project in propel. Most time this leads to insufficient information about deals and product sold.
In big project I need to keep asking various information and documents to PM now and than. This make me to look for an idea.
Solution Approach:
Below information should be make mandatory in resource request.
Sales order number
SE name
Account Rep Name
RPQ / Qualifier if required
Customer contact details
As a result, delivery team and PM team can save time and reduce email back and forth for details required from Sales.
