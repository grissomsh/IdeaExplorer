Mobile Chat Tool

It is always better when you have a "kind of" more personal relation with a colleague rather than emails and on Office Communicator. This chatting tool will let you communicate (Out of working hours) with any of your colleagues (specially in other GEOs).
Mobile app that facilitates communication (birthday shout-outs, personal greetings,..etc) between colleagues within same BU but in different GEOs which increases productivity and Great Place To Work factor.
