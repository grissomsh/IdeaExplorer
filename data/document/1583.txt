Rotational Leadership Program 
This program will enhance managing people and leadership skills for the employees.

This program will give an opportunity to the employees to have managerial tasks, Where each employee is chosen each quarter by his manager to take certain roles and be responsible for certain tasks  to be done for the team.
This selection will be based on each employee performance and his ability to take responsibility of such roles.

2-Enhancing the opportunity to take a leadership role during their career path.
