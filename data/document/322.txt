Automation Committe within the COEs
Creating an automation team in each COE that serves its needs.
As a member in the Automation committee in Egypt COE , i though of if we can populate this idea with other EMC COEs. 
Creating Automation committee within other COEs of EMC. This committee includes engineers that have programming skills , people to manage and follow up on projects ,People who are responsible to provide the materials for the projects and responsible for the budget.

This Committee will serve the needs of any projects that the COE needs to implement. 
1-Make use of the talents of the Employees to serve the COE needs.
2-Implement new projects within the COEs with least cost as it is implemented by it's internal Employees.
