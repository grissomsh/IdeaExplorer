EMC Top Trumps

Was as a result of a brain storming session where a twist was put on the original idea to incorporate the challenge set out
Just like the game, each product will have a rating out of 10 under different headings. For example (more headings can be used)

Xtreme I/O Charger
Cloud 10
Speed 9
Security 9
Big data 5
 
The player then picks the heading that they think will score higher than the equivalent heading on the opposite players card.

This will then educate the player on the best EMC product for a particular heading
