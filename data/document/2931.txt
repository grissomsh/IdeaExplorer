Emc Recruitment mobile app 
This idea will help people view and apply for jobs within EMC on the move.
As we drive to promote EMC, and attract the best people and go more mobile, I was thinking why not put the three together, promote attract best talent and go mobile.
My idea is to have an EMC Recruitment mobile app which can be downloaded on to all mobile devices so that people can view and apply for jobs within EMC when on the move. It also should have simple technology for applying and linking resumes/CVs. This will promote EMC going forward to the journey to the 3rd platform. The world is moving to mobile and in order for EMC to attract the best people we need to be mobile. This can be promoted on events globally and in colleges schools  plant tours etc. 
1.	Promoting EMC brand .
2.	Attracting best people .
3.	Have easy view and apply for jobs when mobile .
