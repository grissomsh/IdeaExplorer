EMCsquare (Virtual Tour)
Making use of crowd sourcing technology to make it easier for emc employees to travel within the world in case of business visit or touristic one
Foursquare app.
We will have a mobile app which can be accessed from multiple platforms such as Android and iOS.

The application will be categorized by country and each country has multiple categories such as: restaurants, hotels, places to visit and services.

Each employee will have his own profile and he can add entries in each of the categories.
Entries will contain pictures, his feedback and rating.

Other employees can as also have the ability to comment on these entries.
-Making life easy for EMC employees to travel to other countries whether it was business visit or touristic visit.
