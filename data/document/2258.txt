Micro Innovation forum
A COE level micro innovation forum
This idea is to encourage more internal productivity improvements among the team members and recognize and celebrate their efforts.
On a COE or BU level, the leaders can have a micro innovation day where the leaders can walk to team cubicles and the team members can talk or showcase some of the micro innovations done by them. the leaders can then present these micro innovators with a memento to recognize their micro innovation
Helps innovation and thinking out the box. Will support the GPTW initiative.
