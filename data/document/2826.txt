Employment for Veterans
EMC should support veterans and injured veterans with opportunities to build professional network. EMC should value the development and profession growth of veterans.
Idea came up on reading and valuing the services that offers on technology industry.
1. As a Veteran, they have a unique combination of exceptional talent, experience and leadership skills.
2. Veteran's commitment to excellence and dedicated service can bring outstanding success at EMC.
3. Discipline and Skills that Veterans strengthened in Military are valuable in technology growing Industry.
4. Veterans can be successful Software Developer, QA Engineer, Industrial Production Engineer and Administrative services Manager.
This would be much helpful for EMC's growth in the industry and adds value to the company.
