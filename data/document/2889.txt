Account Manager communication
The challenge is about any idea in your departement making your work flow easier ,simpler and more efficient.
the Idea came up when i started to figure out that i have a lot of cases opened and i cant do any progress in them as there are no account managers to reply for these cases and i have a deadline to close the case.
When the tech refresh team assign parties to our asc support to check them out and the ASC Support create cases afterwards , the tech refresh team should assign the account manager for this case to provide us with the whole data we will need,and there must be a deadline for their response and if there is no co-operation we shall have the authority to escalate them 
When account managers reply from the start ,we will do more progress in our work , productivity will be more as more cases will be closed in less time .
