Store and Relax
By using this idea EMC can create a space for itself in new domain which can turn in to a Billion dollar product.
Brain-stormed with co-inventor
Our solution will work in following ways:
1.Currently almost every person has their data stored on laptop,harddisk,phone etc.
2.As we know, "Needs of storing data getting increased everyday, so the demand for disks will increase to store it".
3.Currently people don't want to delete the data from their laptop/hardisk though they are not using it as they might need them in future.
4.So  to tackle this problem, we can provide service to people such that they can store their data at out datacenter & start utilizing their storage (laptop/harddisks) again.
5.here we can provide door step service to take data from customers & copy it to our datacenter .
6.to make security to users data, we can provide encryption (public & private).
7.Once all data is available at our datacenter we can provide the online portal to respective customer from where they can access their data by using private key & also download & upload whatever data they required.
8.Also if customer needs huge amount of data from the datacenter then we can ship the data at their door step.
9.Here we can apply different solution like de-duplication(at our datacenter site) so that less storage used as many of customers will be having same data such  as movies,songs etc.
10.here we can charge customer on monthly basis to get revenue generated for longer time.

11.Also we can make quite a few conclusions by doing Data Analysis (Hadoop technology)their type of secured data.
