Sensors in laptop/Desktop to monitor user activity.
In coming years we need to move  from smarter thinking to smartest  thinking. And  IOT devices , around us could be of great use for this. For this we need to make such devices which should be capable of handling human behavior to a great extent.
This is part of Innovation Mining workshop.
The proposed device will be based on ECA(Event, Condition and Action). These devices will be attached with IT equipments such as laptops/monitors. Device will be capturing the user's activity(so called Events). And bsed on condition , suggest for appropriate action.
Its next generation solution, which might not available in the market by now. The intended device/soution would  be a great driver for the next generation's smartest cities plan.
