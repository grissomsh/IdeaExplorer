Tools requesting portal
It will convert unorganized process for each new employee when their managers request access to new tools and make it easier using IT technology.
As a TSE in CS, Managers have to request each tool on its own separate portal and it is hard to keep track of each request.
-Having a centralized portal where employees and their managers can view the request of each tool and the status of the request.
-Having ETA as well can be beneficial to know the expected time for each request.
-Saving employees time
-Saving managers time
-Using IT to enhance operations of employees
-Making the whole process smooth and easy
