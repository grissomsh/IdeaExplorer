Smart traffic Signal
Taking advantage of big data, data mining and AI, the system could reduce traffic jam according to the time, location and the traffic load
2015/3
The signals on the crossing allow the cars to get into only when the roads has enough capacity, otherwise, it always show "stop" lights.
All signals in the city forms a big network. The system can lead the traffic according to the real-time traffic load, location, time, traffic trend in history , customized policies and so on.
At the same time, the real-time traffic status can be exported to navigators. With it, drivers can avoid traffic jam.
The traffic jam could be reduced and navigators can utilize the real-time traffic status.
