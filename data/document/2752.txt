Customer Loyalty Programm (points collection)
This Idea will build a loyal relationship between the customers and EMC. As well change the whole experience that customer passes through with EMC.

From the experience of working in Airline, loyalty collecting points program has been very successful. As people will choose the airline even if it is more expensive to gain the points and use it in other things.

Create customer incentive program (Points Collection) with new purchases, upgrades, deals. so they can redeem these points with things that EMC can offer them such as (seats in technical tranings for their system adminstrators), so they will prefer to go with EMC than any other competitor as we will offer them added value
We will keep customer loyal to EMC by giving them added value to their purchases so they will be hardly switching to other competitor.
