Know your team and profile portfolio application
Better employee satisfaction is important for our organisations success. Are we really making all the efforts to know our employees. If not its a big challenge for our organisation as our employees are the biggest asset.
Thought came in as part of the employee empowerment and engagement 
A tool developed in house to track 1:1 meeting, IDP, aspiration, experience, skill set, certification, strengths, development area, ideas, what they can do for the company, task delegations etc in one central tool. 
Know your employee better, good gap analysis, help the employee to be successful, hear their feedback in a timely manner.
