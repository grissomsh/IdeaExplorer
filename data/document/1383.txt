Innovative Induction Day 
This relates to the below two challenges. 
New hires have been providing some feedback about the induction day within the department. The feedback entailed some comments that it is not engaging enough and sometimes they did not follow what was being said. 
To make the induction day more innovative and more engaging. The day should be divided into parts where new hires can have a tour in the departments and meet all the team managers and get to see the lab and how people work.

Also, break apart the presentation part of the day and make it more engaging and include more visuals about what EMC does.  
More engaging introduction.
New hires will get to see the floors and the teams and the managers.
Will get to see the lab and the equipment. 
New hires will be more exciting.
