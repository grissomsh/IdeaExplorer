Real-time display of Bus waiting time at bus stops
This idea is intended to solve the transportation problems in big cities. Convenient transportation is an important part of smart cities.
The idea is discussed at a group meeting.
Install an electronic board at bus stops, which will display the bus arrival time. 
with the real-time display of bus waiting time at bus stops, commuters will be able to plan their time more reasonably and conveniently, readuce time wasting.
