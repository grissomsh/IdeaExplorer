Meen Fain?

From the travel experience I had and how it will be good to know who is where and if they share same interests
Meen Fain? will act as an internal social network, it could be integrated with any of EMC's portals. Any user will be creating a profile with details on Location, BU, Interests and His/her willingness to mingle with other EMCians. 
Whenever anyone is traveling to any location, s/he will be getting notification of other people in that location, with their interest and willgness to meet up. Also the App will provide communication tools like messages.
