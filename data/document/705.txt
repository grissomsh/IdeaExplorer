Car Camera viewing corners
Using technology towards smart-city.
Seeing a lot of accidents happening due to drivers losing sight when they try to pass a cross street.
-Having a camera in front grill which can have a 180 degree view.
-Camera will be connected to screen inside the car so driver can check them.
-Camera can record and save the view in case anything happens and the driver want to view it later.
-Making it easy and safe for car drivers to pass cross streets to avoid collision.
-Saving peoples lives
