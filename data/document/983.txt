Innovation challenges for universities 
We are working towards having diversity in innovation teams. If we do apply this model, we will be able to enhance overall innovation and make new opportunities.
TED talk.
EMC universities relation committee and EMC innovation committee will be both cooperating to make events in selected universities.

In each Event, EMC Representative will be discussing the current future challenges that we need to address.

After the discussion, Attendees will be able to brainstorm together and provide their ideas to EMC committee where they will select winners and provide them with awards.
Success would be seeing more people participating and helping EMC to be more innovative and participant in that kind of events.
