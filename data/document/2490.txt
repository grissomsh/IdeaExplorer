Continuous gathering of Traffic statistics
Data Storage & Big Data use in Smart City
The idea came into my mind through seeing, in countries where traffic is a big issue, ambulances and fire fighting trucks being stuck in traffic at times where their help is needed.
Through sensors implemented in roads, an estimate of the average speeds of cars can be determined and reported to servers.
Based on big data and analytics, ambulances, fire fighters & police officers can get information on the best roads to take to reach the area for which assistance is needed.
The idea would be successful when the help of civil forces are sought in timely manner.
